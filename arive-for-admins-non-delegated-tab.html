ARIVE for Admins | Non-Delegated Tab

Author: Corey Burns | Updated: October 2025

Overview

This ARIVE for Admins tutorial focuses on the Non-Delegated Tab within the loan origination system's company settings. It is designed to guide administrators through configuring and managing settings specific to non-delegated correspondent lending workflows. Viewers will learn how to navigate and utilize these features to optimize their company's operations.

Key Takeaways

  • The Non-Delegated Tab appears in Company Settings once your account is enabled for non-delegated lending.
  • Configure default pricing channels (Broker or Non-Del) to streamline your most common loan scenarios.
  • Set up margin rules to adjust pricing on non-delegated loans based on specific criteria.
  • Input your company's mortgagee clause, which will be automatically applied to all non-delegated loan files.
  • Manage warehouse bank rules and MERS MIN generation for non-delegated loans directly within the admin settings.

Video Chapters

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is non-delegated correspondent lending?
    A: In non-delegated correspondent lending, the mortgage originator handles the application, closing, and funding, but the investor or loan buyer is responsible for the underwriting.

    Q: Where do I find the Non-Delegated tab in ARIVE?
    A: The Non-Delegated tab is located in your Company Settings, accessible by company and associate admins, but it only appears for accounts on a Non-Del subscription plan.

    Q: Can I switch between Broker and Non-Delegated channels in ARIVE?
    A: Yes, if enabled by an administrator in the Pricing Setup, users can be given the option to override the default and select either the Broker or Non-Del channel within the Product & Pricing Engine.
