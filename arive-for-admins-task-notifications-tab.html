ARIVE for Admins | Task Notifications Tab

Author: Corey Burns | Updated: October 2025

Overview

This video tutorial focuses on the Task Notifications Tab within the ARIVE Loan Origination System's admin controls. It provides a comprehensive guide for administrators on how to effectively set up and manage task-related notifications for their team, ensuring streamlined communication and workflow.

Key Takeaways

  • The Task Notifications Tab is located within the Company Settings under Admin Controls.
  • You can create custom notifications for specific tasks within the loan process.
  • Notifications can be configured to be sent to different roles or specific users.
  • Automated task notifications help ensure deadlines are met and improve team communication.
  • Properly configuring these settings is key to optimizing workflow in the ARIVE LOS.

Video Chapters

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: Where do I find the Task Notifications Tab in ARIVE?
    A: You can find the Task Notifications Tab in the admin section by navigating to Company Settings and then selecting it from the available tabs.

    Q: Can I customize who receives a specific task notification?
    A: Yes, ARIVE allows administrators to customize notification recipients. You can assign alerts to be sent to specific user roles (like loan officers or processors) or to individual users within the system.
