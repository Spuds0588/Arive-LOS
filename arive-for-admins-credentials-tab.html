ARIVE for Admins | Credentials Tab

Author: Corey Burns | Updated: October 2025

Overview

This video is a detailed tutorial for administrators of the ARIVE Loan Origination System (LOS). It specifically focuses on navigating and managing the Credentials Tab within the main company settings, which is crucial for handling third-party service integrations.

Key Takeaways

  • The Credentials Tab is the central location for managing all third-party credentials within ARIVE.
  • Administrators can add, view, and manage credentials for various integrated services directly from this tab.
  • Properly configured credentials ensure seamless integration with essential mortgage services.
  • This section allows you to control which users have access to specific integrated platforms.
  • Keeping credentials updated in this tab is vital for uninterrupted system functionality.

Video Chapters

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is the purpose of the Credentials Tab in ARIVE for admins?
    A: The Credentials Tab allows administrators to securely store and manage the login information for various third-party services that integrate with the ARIVE Loan Origination System, such as credit reporting agencies and automated underwriting systems.

    Q: Who can access and manage the Credentials Tab in ARIVE?
    A: Typically, only users with administrative-level permissions can access the company settings to manage the information within the Credentials Tab.

    Q: Where can I find the Credentials Tab in the ARIVE system?
    A: The Credentials Tab is located within the main Company Settings section of the ARIVE platform, accessible to users with administrative privileges.
