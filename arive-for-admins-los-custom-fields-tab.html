ARIVE for Admins | LOS Custom Fields Tab

Author: Corey Burns | Updated: October 2025

Overview

For administrators of the ARIVE Loan Origination System (LOS), the ability to customize data fields is crucial for adapting to specific operational needs. This tutorial focuses on the LOS Custom Fields Tab located within the ARIVE Admin Controls. The feature allows administrators to create new data fields that are not natively available in the system, ensuring that all necessary information for unique loan products or internal processes is captured directly within the loan file. These custom fields can be strategically placed on different tabs within a loan, such as 'Loan and Property' or 'Loan Center,' and can be configured to appear only for specific loan purposes or mortgage types, providing a tailored and efficient user experience.

Key Takeaways

  • Administrators can add custom data fields to capture information not natively supported by the ARIVE LOS.
  • Custom fields can be assigned to specific tabs within a loan file, like 'Loan and Property' or 'Loan Center'.
  • Visibility of custom fields can be restricted to particular loan purposes (e.g., Purchase, Refinance) or specific mortgage types.
  • Role-based access and editing permissions can be configured for custom fields, including conditional access based on loan status.
  • Data from custom fields can be included in reports, allowing for more comprehensive analysis and workflow management.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is the purpose of the LOS Custom Fields Tab in ARIVE?
    A: The LOS Custom Fields Tab allows company administrators to create and manage custom data fields within the ARIVE Loan Origination System to capture specific information that isn't included in the standard setup.

    Q: Who has permission to create and manage LOS Custom Fields in ARIVE?
    A: Company Administrators have the primary access and permissions to create and configure custom fields through the 'Admin Controls' section in the company settings.

    Q: Can I control where and when a custom field appears in a loan file?
    A: Yes, administrators can choose to display a custom field on either the 'Loan and Property' or 'Loan Center' tab. You can also set conditions for it to appear only on loans with a specific purpose (like purchase or refinance) or mortgage type.
