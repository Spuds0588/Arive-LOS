Overview

For mortgage professionals, starting the day with a clear plan is crucial for success. The Arive loan origination system (LOS) offers a powerful feature to enhance daily productivity: an automated daily task email. This email delivers a concise and actionable to-do list directly to your inbox each morning. By consolidating your most pressing tasks, it eliminates the need to manually sort through files, allowing you to focus on high-priority items and manage your mortgage workflow with greater efficiency.

Key Takeaways

  • Arive sends an automated daily email that functions as your to-do list.
  • The tasks are pulled directly from your Loan Origination System (LOS).
  • This feature helps you prioritize your day without manual guesswork.
  • Using the daily task email can significantly improve your mortgage business efficiency.
