Overview

This tutorial addresses a frequent issue encountered by users of the Arive Point of Sale (POS) system: the 'Email Already Used' error. This error typically occurs when a contact's email address is already associated with another record within the platform, preventing the creation of a new file. The guide walks through the specific steps required to identify the duplicate entry and resolve the conflict, allowing mortgage professionals to efficiently manage their client data and maintain a smooth workflow.

Key Takeaways

  • The 'Email Already Used' error in Arive indicates an email is already tied to another contact or loan file in the system.
  • Resolving the error requires searching the Arive platform to locate the original record associated with the email address.
  • The fix involves either updating or disassociating the email from the conflicting record to free it up for new use.
  • Properly managing contact data helps prevent this error, ensuring a more efficient loan origination process.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What causes the 'Email Already Used' error in Arive POS?
    A: This error occurs because the email address you are trying to use is already linked to another contact, such as a borrower or co-borrower, in your Arive database, either in an active or archived loan file.

    Q: Do I need to delete the old contact to fix the 'Email Already Used' error?
    A: No, it's not always necessary to delete the contact. The best practice is to first search for the email to find the existing record. From there, you can merge duplicate contacts or update the existing file as needed to resolve the conflict.

    Q: Will this fix affect my archived loan files in Arive?
    A: The error can originate from both active and archived files. The solution involves finding the source of the conflict, which may be in an archived record, and making the necessary correction without negatively impacting your historical data.
