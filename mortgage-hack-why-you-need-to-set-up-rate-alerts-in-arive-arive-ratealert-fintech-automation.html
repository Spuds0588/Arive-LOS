Mortgage Hack: Why You NEED to Set Up Rate Alerts in Arive! #Arive #RateAlert #Fintech #Automation

Author: Corey Burns | Updated: October 2025

Overview

This video explains the importance and functionality of setting up rate alerts within the Arive mortgage platform. It demonstrates how this feature can automatically notify brokers when a past client's interest rate is significantly higher than current market rates, creating an opportunity for a refinance conversation before the client shops around.

Key Takeaways

  • Set up rate alerts in Arive to monitor past clients' mortgages.
  • Automated alerts notify you when a client can benefit from a lower market rate.
  • Proactively contacting clients for refinances builds loyalty and business.
  • This feature helps you compete with other lenders who may target your clients.
  • Rate alerts are a powerful, underutilized tool for client retention.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is the main benefit of using rate alerts in Arive?
    A: The main benefit is that the system automatically identifies refinance opportunities in your database, allowing you to proactively reach out to past clients before they start looking elsewhere.

    Q: How do Arive rate alerts help mortgage brokers retain clients?
    A: By notifying you when a past client could save money with a new rate, it allows you to provide value and secure their business for a refinance, preventing them from going to a competitor.
