ARIVE for Admins | Plans & Billing Tab

Author: Corey Burns | Updated: October 2025

Overview

For administrators of the ARIVE Loan Origination System (LOS), the Plans & Billing tab is a crucial component of company management. This section within the Admin Controls provides a centralized dashboard for overseeing your organization's subscription details. Here, administrators can view the current plan, manage the number of user licenses, handle add-ons like social media management, and update credit card information. It also serves as a repository for all billing history, allowing for easy access to past invoices and payment records.

Key Takeaways

  • Locate and understand your company's current ARIVE subscription plan.
  • Effectively manage user licenses and optional add-on features.
  • Update payment methods and access your complete billing history.
  • Navigate the key functions within the ARIVE Admin Plans & Billing tab.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: Where can I find my company's billing history in ARIVE?
    A: Your company's complete billing history, including past invoices and payment details, is accessible to administrators within the 'Plans & Billing' tab in the main Admin Controls section.

    Q: How do I add or remove user licenses in the ARIVE platform?
    A: Administrators can add or remove user licenses directly from the Plans & Billing tab. This control panel allows you to adjust the number of active users associated with your company's subscription.

    Q: Can I change my company's credit card information within ARIVE?
    A: Yes, the Plans & Billing section allows designated administrators to securely update the credit card information used for subscription payments and other billable services.
